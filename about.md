Hi! I'm Gen. I used to make websites about business, back when I lived on a planet with fewer slimes on it. Now I live on Goopiter and I make websites about slimes.

The slimes are fine. They're friendly. They're cute. They don't fully understand me, and I don't fully understand them, but they help me stay healthy and connected and I don't feel like I should complain too much about their hospitality. It's just kind of boring out here on my own.

Goo Grotto - the website - is my tribute to them. If you find a slime you like and have a little fun, that's the point. If you find a slime you love, that's even better: that's you tapping into the Meld alongside me and my little buddies. Don't be alarmed! They like it too. 😊
